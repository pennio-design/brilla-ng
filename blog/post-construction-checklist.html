Post-Construction Cleaning Checklist: Before & After Renovation

Renovating your home or office is exciting — but the mess afterward? Not so much. Dust, debris, and hidden hazards can ruin your fresh new space. Here's a checklist to help you clean efficiently and safely.

Why Post-Construction Cleaning Is Crucial

It's not just about aesthetics — leftover construction debris can pose health and safety risks. That’s why hiring professionals like Brilla NG is often the smartest move.

Before You Start: Essential Tools

  • Heavy-duty vacuum with HEPA filter
  • Microfiber cloths & mops
  • Dust masks & gloves
  • Safe surface disinfectants

Room-by-Room Checklist

1. Living Room & Bedrooms

  • Dust ceiling fans, lights, and vents
  • Wipe walls, baseboards, and window sills
  • Vacuum floors and carpets thoroughly

2. Kitchen

  • Clean all cabinets (inside & out)
  • Wipe down countertops and appliances
  • Sanitize sinks and polish faucets

3. Bathroom

  • Scrub tiles, grout, and fixtures
  • Remove dust from exhaust fans
  • Clean mirrors, windows, and floors

4. Outdoor & Entryways

  • Sweep patios and entry paths
  • Clear dust from exterior windows and doors
  • Ensure all construction waste is disposed of properly
